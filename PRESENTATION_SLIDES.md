### Key Talking Points:
1. **Emphasize the scale of the problem** - Use the big numbers
2. **Highlight AI capabilities** - This differentiates EcoBite
3. **Show multi-stakeholder value** - Not just for consumers
4. **Connect to SDGs** - Shows alignment with global goals
5. **End with clear call to action** - What can they do?

### Visual Recommendations:
- Use screenshots from the actual app
- Include charts/graphs showing impact metrics
- Show the OCR scanning in action
- Display the SDG score dashboard
- Feature community success stories

### Delivery Tips:
- **Speak clearly** at ~150 words/minute
- **Pause** after key statistics
- **Make eye contact** with the audience
- **Use hand gestures** to emphasize points
- **End with energy** - this is an exciting solution!
