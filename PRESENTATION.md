# EcoBite: 2-Minute Presentation Script

## Introduction (15 seconds)
"Good [morning/afternoon]. Today I'm excited to present **EcoBite** - a comprehensive food waste management platform that transforms how households, businesses, and communities tackle food waste while contributing to the UN Sustainable Development Goals."

---

## The Problem (20 seconds)
"Globally, we waste 1.3 billion tons of food annually - that's one-third of all food produced. This waste costs $1 trillion and generates 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions. Current solutions are fragmented and don't address the entire food lifecycle from purchase to disposal."

---

## Our Solution (25 seconds)
"EcoBite is an integrated platform that uses AI and smart technology to prevent food waste at every stage. We connect households, businesses, restaurants, farmers, and NGOs in a circular economy that turns waste into value. Our platform tracks food from purchase to consumption, predicts waste, optimizes meals, and redirects surplus to those who need it."

---

## Key Features (45 seconds)

**Smart Kitchen Module:**
- OCR-powered pantry scanning for instant inventory tracking
- AI-driven consumption pattern analysis and waste prediction
- Automated meal planning that uses expiring items first
- NourishBot - an AI chatbot for nutrition and waste reduction advice
- SDG Impact scoring to measure your sustainability impact

**Marketplace & Community:**
- Surplus food listing and sharing platform
- Business-to-business surplus trading
- Direct connections between farmers and buyers
- NGO donation integration for food redistribution
- Community hub for sharing recipes and tips

**Advanced Analytics:**
- Real-time waste estimation and projections
- Nutrient gap analysis for better nutrition
- Flow network visualization of food supply chains
- Bio-transformation tracking for composting and energy conversion

**Gamification:**
- Points and rewards system for sustainable actions
- Leaderboards and achievements
- CO₂ savings tracking
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, French, Bengali)

---

## Impact & Technology (10 seconds)
"EcoBite uses React, TypeScript, and modern AI to deliver a beautiful, responsive experience. Our platform supports multiple user types - households, businesses, restaurants, and government entities - all working together to reduce waste and improve food security."

---

## Call to Action (5 seconds)
"EcoBite isn't just an app - it's a movement toward zero food waste. Join us in building a sustainable future, one meal at a time. Thank you."

---

## Presentation Notes:
